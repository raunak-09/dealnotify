FAQ

Common questions

Does PriceGuard work on Amazon? +
Yes — Amazon is one of our most popular supported stores. Just paste any Amazon product URL and set your target price. We'll monitor it and alert you when the price drops.
Do I need to install anything? +
No downloads, no browser extensions, no apps. PriceGuard is entirely web-based. Just sign up, paste a URL, and we do the rest from our servers.
How often does PriceGuard check prices? +
We check every hour, 24 hours a day. Most flash sales and price changes last only a few hours, so our hourly cadence means you'll almost never miss a deal.
What happens after my free trial ends? +
Your monitoring simply pauses — we never charge you automatically. If you want to continue, you can upgrade to Pro for $4.99/month. If not, no worries and no charge.
Can I cancel my Pro plan at any time? +
Absolutely. Cancel any time from your dashboard with one click. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no questions asked.
Is my email address safe with PriceGuard? +
Yes. We use your email only to send you price drop alerts and account updates. We never sell or share your email with third parties, ever.
