1 Who We Are

DealNotify is a price monitoring and restock alert service operated at dealnotify.co. We help users track product prices and stock availability on major online retailers and receive email notifications when prices drop or items come back in stock.

2 Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and password (hashed and salted — we never store plaintext passwords) when you create an account.
  • Product tracking data: The product URLs you choose to track, your target prices, and the type of alert you select (price drop or restock).
  • Payment information: If you subscribe to the Pro plan, payment is processed entirely by Stripe. We do not store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details on our servers. We only retain your Stripe customer ID to manage your subscription.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics data collected via Google Analytics, including pages visited, browser type, and general geographic region. This data is anonymous and aggregated.

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the price monitoring and restock alert service
  • Send you email notifications when a tracked product meets your alert criteria
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Respond to support requests submitted via our contact form
  • Send occasional product updates or newsletters (only if you opted in during signup — you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve the service through anonymous, aggregated usage analytics

4 Chrome Extension Data

The DealNotify Chrome extension collects the following data when you use it:

  • Active tab URL: When you click the extension icon, it reads the URL of your current tab to detect if you are on a supported retailer (Amazon, Walmart, Best Buy, Target, eBay, or Costco). This information is only used locally to display product information in the extension popup.
  • Product information: The extension reads the product title and current price from the page using content scripts. This data is only sent to our server when you explicitly click "Start Tracking" or "Alert Me When Back in Stock."
  • Authentication token: Your login session token is stored locally in chrome.storage.local (sandboxed to the extension) and is sent to our API via secure HTTPS headers to authenticate requests.

The extension does not track your browsing history, does not run on pages other than the six supported retailer domains, does not collect any data in the background, and does not modify any web page content.

5 Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by Railway. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Passwords are hashed and salted before storage — we cannot read your password
  • Authentication tokens are sent via secure HTTP headers (Authorization: Bearer), never in URLs
  • Admin endpoints are protected with constant-time password comparison to prevent timing attacks
  • Rate limiting is in place on login, signup, and contact endpoints to prevent abuse
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers are set to prevent cross-site scripting

While we take security seriously, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

6 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate DealNotify:

  • Stripe — for secure payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy: stripe.com/privacy
  • SendGrid — for sending email notifications. SendGrid's privacy policy: twilio.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Analytics — for anonymous usage analytics. Google's privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firecrawl — for scraping product prices from retailer websites on your behalf
  • Railway — for hosting our application and database

We do not share your personal information with these services beyond what is necessary for them to perform their function (e.g., your email is shared with SendGrid solely to deliver your alerts).

7 Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how visitors use the site. This helps us understand traffic patterns and improve the user experience. Google Analytics may set cookies in your browser.

We do not use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or tracking across other websites. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

8 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Period.

We will only disclose your information if required to do so by law, such as in response to a valid subpoena, court order, or government request.

9 Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information from our active databases within 30 days. Some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days before being permanently deleted.

Price history and stock check data associated with your tracked products is retained for the duration of your account to provide you with historical charts and trends.

10 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information in your account
  • Delete your account and personal data
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time by clicking "unsubscribe" or emailing us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@dealnotify.co. We will respond within 14 business days.

11 Children's Privacy

DealNotify is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have inadvertently collected data from a minor, we will promptly delete it.

12 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify registered users by email of any material changes at least 14 days before they take effect. The "Effective Date" at the top of this page will be updated accordingly.
